Remedial Therapy




   Remedial Therapy utilises many techniques, including massage, to   address a wide range of soft tissue problems. The aim is to reduce pain   and tension by releasing pressure of muscles in spasm, and to address   tendon and ligament complaints.

  For the children diagnosed with ADHD, Learning disability and slow   learners are given remedial therapy. The classes are conducted after   school hours.
